Welcome!

This is an HCI (Human-Computer Interaction) experiment conducted by Zhu Duoduo for an assignment in CS4249 Phenomena and Theories of Human-Computer Interaction, National University of Singapore.

The purpose of this experiment is to gather information about performances of two copy-paste techniques under different scenarios. The two techniques are Ctrl-C Ctrl-V keyboard shortcut and a new technique named AutoComPaste.

Participants are required to complete copy-paste tasks according to the instructions given. More details will be provided in the "Instructions" section later.

Please write your participant number ID given by your experimenter. Participant IDs should only consist of numeric characters.
